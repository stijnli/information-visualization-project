Below, you can see a graph chart that allows you to explore the relationship between different songs, artists, albums and genres. Left-clicking on nodes opens and closes their children, while right-clicking on songs (only circles) adds them to the selected song list.

In the rank chart, you can see the ranking in the top 50, globally or in a specific country. You can use the dropdown to select a country.

The heatmap below shows the audio features. Luminance reflects the feature's value in range of the selection, with exact values visible by hovering over the headers or elements
